about me

Hey there! I'm Maya Quandt, an art and design student at Marian University in Indianapolis, IN. I'm currently a junior studying graphic design, with minors in communication and art history. I'd consider myself an avid learner, experimenter, and adventurer. I love to try new things - in the art + design world, among others. I am very passionate about what I do, and I'm always up for a challenge.

I hold myself to a high standard when creating and designing, and I especially focus on being flexible, simplistic, efficient, innovative, and responsible. I really enjoy making others happy, and that is a huge part of why I love designing for myself and others. When I'm not creating something new, you can find me outside in nature, playing the piano, or working out.

If you'd like to work together, learn more about what I do, or just ask a few questions, send me a message through my contact form. I'd love to hear from you!

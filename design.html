PORTFOLIO // design

typographic self-portrait

"This project focused on expressively using typographic form/counterform (structure) to create a self-portrait with insight of form and meaning. The central aesthetic objective of this assignment was to create a self-portrait which communicates an insight into your personality through the expressive combination of typographic forms."

type history poster - gotham htf

"For this project, we had to create a poster design which successfully employed typographic/compositional hierarchy and visual interest though the use of a grid system, while understanding the importance of historical research and context to our design process and decisions."

typographic hierarchy

"The purpose of this project was to examine how typographic (visual) hierarchy can impact the reading and comprehension of text by changing typographic parameters (style, weight, size and spacing)."

modular infographic poster

"This project looks at formal (visual) structure as it pertains to the relationship between form and content. We had to explore how content can shape the form of a design, and how form can organize content. The visual organizing principles explored in this project were modularity and the grid."
